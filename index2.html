Scramble Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite food. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scramble eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbd butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in tha bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs fremthe edge of the pan to tha center every 20 second(as ifyou are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are stillmoist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plateuntilled serve)
